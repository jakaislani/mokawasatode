Juan Luis Morera Luna, well known as Wisin (born December 19, 1978), is a reggaeton artist, singer and album producer from Puerto Rico. As a part of the Wisin & Yandel reggaeton duo, he rose to success. His first solo album, El Sobreviviente, was released in 2004, while his second solo studio album, El Regreso del Sobreviviente, was released ten years later, in 2014. Currently, Wisin is concentrating with Prince Royce on their Strength and Love trip. For his distinctive and aggressive style of rapping, Wisin is remembered. Other than this, he has an enormous user base on his social networking websites.
