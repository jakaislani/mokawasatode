Gotabaya Rajapaksa Bio, Size, Age, Female, Wiki, Facts and Net Worth

Gotabhaya Rajapaksa is a famous Sri Lankan politician, former army officer and current president of Sri Lanka. His big brother Mahinda Rajapaksa He was also President of Sri Lanka from 2005 to 2015. Gotabhaya served as Secretary of the Ministry of Defense and Urban Development in the government of his older brother. He is widely recognized for his contribution to the defeat of the Tamil Tigers and the end of the civil war in Sri Lanka.

Gotabhaya Rajapaksa was born on 20 June 1949 in Palatuwa, Matara District, Sri Lanka, to a political family. He attended elementary and high school at Ananda College in Colombo. He then completed his graduate studies in Information Technology at the University of Colombo in 1992.
