Twinkle Bajpai, popularly known as Tia Bajpai, is a multi-skilled person and flaunts her skills, like; acting and singing. She exclusively works in the Bollywood film industry and has acted in various Indian TV series and movies. While she is noted for her performance in the movie “Haunted 3D” which was released in the year 2012. She was born on 23rd May 1989, Lucknow, UP, India where she grew up and studied. She has a younger brother named Naveen Bajpai. She belongs to a Hindu, Brahmin family.

Career

In 2005, Tia Bajpai took the start of her career on the small screen with a TV show “Sa Re Ga Ma” Challenge. It was the beginning of her career when she discovered her interest in singing and decided to build her career in the field of music. Afterward, she made a singing debut with a single “Sau Baras” which she had dropped in the year 2011. In 2012, she made a Bollywood film debut named “Haunted 3D”. Since then, she frequently appeared in various Indian movies and TV series for which she gained huge acclaim. Some of her best movies include “Desi Kattey” which was released in the year 2014, “1920- Evil Returns” which was released in 2012, “Lanka” in 2011 and others that are worth sharing. While her most popular TV series include “Ghar Ki Lakshmi Betiyan” which was aired in the year 2006, “Sa Re Ga Ma challenge” in 2005, “and others are notable. One of her interviews, Tia Bajpai revealed that she always wanted to be an actress and discovered her inclination for acting in her childhood phase. While the “Sa Re Ga Ma” TV show was the reason for her fame through which she gained huge success and appreciation. Moreover, she also made an appearance in another TV show named “Kaun Jeeteyga Bollywood Ka Ticket”. Also, she got various acting gigs and marked her identity in various commercials as well.

Achievements

Among various prestigious achievements, Tia Bajpai was honored with the Mirchi Music Awards for the category of Female Vocalist of the Year, and also she has earned a nomination of the Indian Telly Award for the category of Best Fresh New Face (Female).
